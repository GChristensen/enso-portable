Enso is a quasimodal launcher — tap Caps Lock, type a command, and hit Enter. No windows to hunt for, no menus to click. It runs invisibly until you summon it, turning at any moment into a command line for your whole computer. This modern revival keeps the legendary feel and makes every command fully scriptable, so you can teach it to do anything.

Everything, one keystroke away.
